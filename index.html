About me

My name is Elliott Reyna. I’m a 22 year old emerging software developer with a growing passion for UI/UX design, artificial intelligence, and building technology that genuinely improves people’s lives. I’m currently developing my technical foundation through the Year Up United program, where I focus on application development, coding fundamentals, and industry ready communication and problem solving skills. My long term goal is to grow into the software and AI field specifically in intelligent systems, user centered design, and automated solutions.

My interest in tech goes beyond writing code. I’m energized by the creativity behind UI/UX design, the logic behind clean, intuitive user flows, and the potential of AI to make everyday tasks smarter and more efficient. I enjoy breaking down complex problems, experimenting with new tools, and transforming ideas into experiences that feel seamless and human centered.

Outside of work and school, I’m grounded by the people and moments that matter most. I’m engaged to my high school sweetheart—we’ve been together for five years and she’s been a huge source of support through every step of my journey. My dog, Abby, has been right there with us too; she’s played a big role in keeping me steady, motivated, and grounded on tough days.

As I move toward my upcoming internship and into the early stages of my tech career, I’m focused on opportunities where design, AI, and human centered thinking come together. I’m excited to keep growing, keep building, and contribute to the next generation of intuitive, intelligent technology. If you’re looking for someone who learns fast, thinks creatively, and brings authenticity to their work, I’d love to connect.
